# Estrada EV Hub — Design System

**Tropical modernism.** Clean geometric grids softened by lush organic photography
and warm amber accents — São Paulo's tension between concrete infrastructure and
green canopy. Teal (#00695C) is the primary brand color; amber (#FFAB40) is the
signature accent (kickers, primary CTAs, the scroll-driven charge bar).

- **Display:** Space Grotesk Bold 700 — squared terminals, technical character.
- **Body:** Outfit Regular 400 / Medium 500 — geometric warmth, generous x-height at 16px.
- **Mood:** sustainable · modern · optimistic · urban.
- **Color strategy:** Defined Hybrid — light paper surfaces with dark teal-black hero/footer bands.
